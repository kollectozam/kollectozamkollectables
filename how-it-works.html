HOW IT WORKS

Kollectozam is Brunei's first live claim platform for TCG collectibles. No auctions. No carts. Just fast, direct claim culture.

1
Browse Claim Drops 🃏
Browse all available items across Pokémon, Naruto Kayou, and One Piece TCG. Each card shows its price, condition, and availability status.
2
Hit CLAIM NOW ⚡
Click the CLAIM NOW button on any available item. Fill in your name and WhatsApp number. The item is instantly reserved for you — and blocked from everyone else.
⏱
Timer Starts Immediately
You have a limited window to complete payment:
⚡ Standard Singles — 10 minutes
💎 High-Value Singles — 15 minutes
📦 Naruto Boxes — 15 minutes
💬
Send Payment via WhatsApp
After claiming, WhatsApp opens automatically with your claim details pre-filled. Send your payment proof (Beret screenshot or cash arrangement) before the timer runs out.
5
Admin Confirms & You Collect 📦
Our team verifies your payment and marks the item as SOLD. Arrange pickup or delivery via WhatsApp. The item is yours!
🔄
If Timer Expires...
If payment isn't received in time, your reservation expires automatically. The item re-appears under BACK UP FOR CLAIM — giving the next collector a second chance.
